CAKE:
1)2 and 1/2 cups (340 g) all-purpose flour.
2)1/4 cup (35 g) cornstarch.
3)2 tablespoons(12 g) unsweetened cocoa powder(any kind works).
4)1 teaspoon baking soda.
5)3/4 teaspoon salt.
6)1/2 cup(1 stick/115 g) unsalted butter , softened to room temperature . 
7)2 and 1/8 cups (425 g) granulated sugar. 
8)1 cup (240 ml) vegetable or canola oil.
9) 3 large eggs.
10)2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
11)1 and 1/4 teaspoons distilled wine vinegar .
12)2-3 tablespoons red food colouring .
13)1 cup (240 ml) buttermilk .

CREAM CHEESE FROSTING :
1)16 oz.(450 g) full-fat block cream cheese (not spread) , at room temperature.
2)2 sticks/225 g unsalted butter , softened to room temperature .
3)4 cups (480 g) powdered sugar ,sifted .
4)2 teaspoons vanilla extract .
5)pinch of salt , optional .
